About Kenrui Solutions

Bridging technology and agriculture for a smarter Kenya

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan businesses and farms through innovative technology solutions that drive productivity, efficiency, and sustainable growth across all 47 counties.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading agritech innovator, transforming traditional farming into smart agriculture through accessible digital solutions.

Our Kenyan Story

Founded in Nairobi in 2018, Kenrui Solutions was born from recognizing the untapped potential at the intersection of Kenya's booming tech sector and our agricultural heritage. What began as a small team of three passionate technologists and agronomists has grown into a nationwide service provider trusted by over 500 farms and businesses.

We take pride in developing solutions that understand the unique challenges of:

  • Smallholder farmers in rural areas
  • Agricultural cooperatives
  • Agri-businesses scaling operations
  • Urban enterprises serving the agricultural value chain
